The Bots

How To Play:

Contol Keys:

TEAM YELLOW BOT
1. To move forward, press the 2 on the remote
2. To move backwards, press 8 on the remote
3. To move to the left, press 4 on the remote
4. To move to the right, press 6 on the remote
5. To stop the robot, press 5 on the remote

TEAM GREEN BOT
1. To move forward, press the 1 on the remote
2. To move backwards, press 3 on the remote
3. To move to the left, press 7 on the remote
4. To move to the right, press 9 on the remote
5. To stop the robot, press 100+ on the remote

Rules:

1. You cannot cross the pink line
2. In order to win, you must score one goal
3. Use the remote to naviagate

To Get Started:

1. Position the robots on either inside
2. Plug in the black wire, to turn the robot on
3. Grab the remote and start the game!
